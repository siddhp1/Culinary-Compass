Our Story

The inspiration for our restaurant finding and recommendation web application was born from the shared joy and occasional challenges of exploring new cities with family. Navigating unfamiliar streets and seeking out restaurants that cater to specific dietary preferences became a familiar ritual. Culinary Compass is our solution to simplify this experience. We understand the importance of finding vegetarian-friendly options or that perfect cuisine, and our goal is to make your culinary journey delightful. This platform goes beyond being a tool; it's your personalized guide to tracking restaurant history and receiving recommendations tailored to your tastes. Join us on this flavorful journey, and let Culinary Compass be your companion in discovering memorable dining experiences.

Get Started

Map your culinary experiences.

Create an Account

Go to the register page and create an account. Then login and customize your account on the account page. You can also add any dietary restrictions using the form at the bottom of the page.

Track Your Restaurant Visits

Create a history of all of the restaurants you visited. We'll study this and find out what you love to eat. Also, you can generate a recap of your restaurant history whenever you'd like.

Find New Restaurants

Don't know what to eat? Generate a list of recommended restaurants based on where you are and what you love. We hope that you have an amazing experience with your Culinary Compass!
